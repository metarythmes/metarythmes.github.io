'Metarythmes'

Our first meeting/jam will be next 21 June, 21h at OBORO.

Participants include:

Stephanie Castonguay - Experimenting with low-fi electronics and audio circuit devices. Exploring the traces left by physical phenomena and materiality through kinetic installations and sound. - http://www.spacedataspace.com/

Charles Bicari (Vieux Néant) - Juxtaposing live sound treatment with electronic music to summon ceremonial trans. - https://vieuxneant.bandcamp.com/

Marije Baalman - Artist and researcher/developer based in Amsterdam - working with sound, code, light and interaction. - https://www.marijebaalman.eu/

AVX Lab (VJ BunBun) - projection design for visual mapping, live event production and interactive installations. - http://avx-lab.com/

Olivier Landry-Gagnon - Using a modular synthesizers system, the artist blends into the machine and extracts a frequent human movement. - https://spacecloudsfilter.bandcamp.com/

Rodrigo Velasco (yecto) - Improvising aural visions through live coding. - https://yecto.github.io/
