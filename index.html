'metarythmes' is a series of meetings to collaborate and explore around the concepts 'live and rhythm' through non conventional vocabularies.

We play and connect different processes in one afternoon and then we share the experiments through a jam session to find emergent materialities, creating new dialogues that embraces feedback, imperfection and failure.
